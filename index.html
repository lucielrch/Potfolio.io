About Me

Who am i?

Born on February 12, 2003, I am 21 years old and full of ambitions. Passionate about music, drawing, and work, I have never been a big fan of studies. Therefore, I have given myself the keys to succeed in a more enjoyable way. With several years of experience in the restaurant industry, I have been working alongside my studies and have loved it. I invested in a tattooing training to pave the way for a more interesting future. Thanks to these initiatives, I have met numerous people who have inspired me and fueled my desire to travel. For the upcoming year, I am heading to New Zealand with my best friend to live, work, dance, and tattoo for a year. What are my qualities ?

Hobbies

What I Do?

Drawing

I love drawing, creating designs on various themes for tattoos, or even just sketching whatever comes to my mind.

Dancing

I've been dancing for 7 years. I love learning and creating, and I've been with the same group all these years. I'm going to miss them dearly when I'm in New Zealand.

Music

Ever since I was little, I've had a musical ear. I love finding the rhythm. My mom was a singer, and I was her biggest fan. Since then, I've been playing multiple instruments, singing, composing, doing covers, and I combine this passion with dance.

Being a waitress/ barmaid

Since I was 13 years old, I've been immersed in the restaurant industry and I've always loved it. During my years of study, I worked on the side as a waitress and bartender because it was my time of the week when I could relax and see something other than my notebooks and courses. My project in New Zealand is to continue this work there to fund my projects and my apartment.

My friends

I left home very quickly because I was very independent, and thanks to that, I was able to meet my second family. My best friends have always been there for me; they almost live with me all the time. We work together, celebrate all the important dates together, and I'm even going to travel to New Zealand with one of them.

Tattoos and piercings

I'm a big fan of tattoos and piercings; I have many of them on my body. I'm so passionate about them that I even paid for training to become a tattoo artist, and I tattoo my friends. I would love to find an apprenticeship in a salon in New Zealand to continue pursuing my passion.

Rugby

One of my older brothers was a rugby player, and he pursued his studies in this field. I grew up surrounded by the love for this sport, and I adore going to matches and supporting my favorite teams. When I'm in New Zealand, I will definitely go watch the All Blacks play.

Preparing my trip to New Zealand

One of my new passions is preparing for my future life in New Zealand. I'm organizing all the paperwork, tickets, places to visit, work, accommodation, etc. It's like living a dream with my best friend.
